About Hormone & Bone Support for Women


Hormone and Bone Support for Women 60 Capsules. The product is intended for use by women 50 years and older. Calcium and vitamin D help to reduce the loss of bone mineral in post-menopausal women. Low bone mineral density is a risk factor for osteoporotic bone fractures. The beneficial effect is obtained with a daily intake of at least 1200 mg of calcium and 20g of vitamin D from all sources. Other relevant EFSA approved health claims: - Vitamin B6 contributes to the regulation of hormonal activity; - Zinc amd Magnesium contribute to the maintenance of normal bones; - Vitamin B6 and Zinc contribute to the normal function of the immune system; - Vitamin B6 contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism and to the reduction of tiredness and fatigue.


Directions: Adults take 2 capsules daily with water and food. Do not exceed recommended daily dose
