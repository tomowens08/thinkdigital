Cooking with Sugar Beet Powder

Sugar Beet Powder is a versatile and natural ingredient that can be used in a variety of recipes. It adds a touch of sweetness, vibrant color, and a nutritional boost to your dishes. Here are some creative recipes to make the most of Sugar Beet Powder:

1. Beetroot Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar Beet Powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, Sugar Beet Powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and stir until just mixed.
  4. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat and pour 1/4 cup portions of the batter.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit for a colorful and nutritious breakfast.

2. Beetroot Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Sugar Beet Powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, Sugar Beet Powder, and olive oil in a food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding water as needed for desired consistency.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.

Serve with pita bread or fresh vegetables for a vibrant and healthy snack.

3. Beetroot Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar Beet Powder

Toppings: granola, fresh berries, coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Blend the frozen banana, Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and Sugar Beet Powder until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and add your favorite toppings.

This vibrant smoothie bowl is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients.

These recipes offer a glimpse of the creative possibilities with Sugar Beet Powder. Feel free to experiment and incorporate this natural sweetener into your cooking, whether in baked goods, smoothies, or savory dishes. Enjoy the vibrant color and subtle sweetness it brings to your meals!
