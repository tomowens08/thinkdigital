TOUR and introduction

Owens-Media began as a spin-off on the much successful CMA partnership, it's self a Liverpool website design and new media concern.

CMA being a partnership, OM is a proprietorship with all contracts and design portfolio the responsibility of Mr. T. Owens and staff directly.

Website design is a broad field and you may have heard it described as both design and elsewhere development. Design generally deals with simpler website that have their look and feel as their main promotional elements where as developers will concentrate on complex back end, which are parts of a website that allow it to do things such as store data or perform never before done functions.

A more accurate reason for the distinction though is the use of software. Certain software with which websites are developed have come to dominate the market. Software such as PHP has little design property but can do really dynamic stuff when it comes to manipulating data. Javascript has great properties to do birth but pales to Flash when it come to visual prowess and seamless animation. Also the very simple to use various image editing software can be used to create stunning visual effects that do not in most cases require improvement.

Because of this, areas of expertise and training of various persons involved, the specialities are fixed.

OM does both of these specialising in both PHP and Flash so incorporating the best of all fields. There is no limit to what OM can do, pushing the boundaries of what has been done before.
